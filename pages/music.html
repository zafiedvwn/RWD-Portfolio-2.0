Music Albums

Weezer's Blue Album is a debut studio album by American rock band Weezer, released on May 10, 1994, by DGC Records. Weezer was formed on February 14, 1992, Los Angeles by singer and guitarist Rivers Cuomo, drummer Patrick Wilson, bassist Matt Sharp, and guitarist Jason Cropper(former founding member, and now has Brian Bell for rhythm guitar). This one is a classic album for me, I've been listening on this one since forever. This band was also the reason why I was hooked with rock music. Great band!
Unknown Pleasures is the debut studio album by English rock band Joy Division, released on 15 June 1979 by Factory Records. Joy Division formed in Salford in 1976 by drummer Terry Mason, guitarist Bernard Sumner, bassist Peter Hook and singer Ian Curtis whom Sumner and Hook already knew, applied for the band and, without having to audition, was taken on. This album and this band saved me during the pandemic, it just speaks so much of how I felt at the time. Emo era, alert!
The Story Begins is the debut extended play by South Korean girl group Twice. It was released by JYP Entertainment on October 20, 2015, through KT Music. Twice consist of members known for their stage name Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, Momo and Tzuyu. I picked this group and album as a representation for my love with K-POP.
